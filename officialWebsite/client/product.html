IPTV Delivery
IPTV is the method of distributing content over a fully managed network. The protocol used in streaming the content is Internet Protocol, hence the name Internet Protocol Television. Though Internet Protocol is used, it is only a private internet and cannot be accessible externally. The video contents are distributed over the private network and can be accessible by only those devices given by operators.
OTT Delivery
In contrast, OTT involves distributing content over the internet which can be accessible publicly. Any devices such as smartphones, tabs and smart tvs which has access to the internet can operate the OTT video stream in addition to the standard set top boxes. So the contents or video streams can be accessible whenever and wherever according to the user’s desire.
